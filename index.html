helium-tax

Calculate Helium hotspot USD income in 2020 (useful for taxes).
Uses 2020 as defined by location of hotspot's most recently asserted location.

Will download a .csv with two columns:

  1. Time of reward in time zone of hotspot's most recently asserted location.
  2. Rewards in USD as given by HNT oracle price at time of reward.
